FAQ

What blockchains does Alphgraphs support?

Alphgraphs currently supports Ethereum, Solana, Polygon, BNB Chain, and Avalanche. We are actively working on adding support for additional chains including Base, Optimism, and Arbitrum. New chains are added based on community demand and ecosystem growth.

Is my wallet data secure?

Absolutely. Alphgraphs is a read-only analytics platform. We never ask for private keys or seed phrases. All data is sourced from public blockchain data. You can track any wallet simply by entering its public address — no wallet connection required.

How real-time is the data?

Pro and Enterprise plans receive real-time data with sub-second updates on price changes, sales, and wallet activity. The Free plan receives data with a 5-minute delay. Our infrastructure processes millions of on-chain events per day to keep you ahead of the market.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. There are no cancellation fees or long-term commitments. Your plan stays active until the end of the current billing period, then automatically reverts to the Free tier.
